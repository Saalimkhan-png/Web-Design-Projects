Agile Development:


Now a days software is used in every sector. We need close relationship with customer or client. We
want to talk to customer and other stakeholders frequently to get new requirements. This is called
agile development. We use a method called Scrum to talk to customer and update customer on almost per
day basis.
what is Scrum?
A scrum is talking to customer and other stakeholders in one month (2-4 weeks) called Sprint. There
is Product Owner And Scrum Master and other developers to talk to customer, take changes and
implement in 1 month.
If we have 6 months project, we will have 6 sprints. Every sprint is a workable and useable product.
Customer can use it and tell what should we do next.
In modern software engineering, Agile is the main methodology to communicate with customer.

Difference Between Software engineer And Software Developer:


Software engineering and software development are closely related fields, and the terms are often
used interchangeably. However, there are some subtle differences between the two:



Focus: Software engineering typically involves a more holistic approach to software development. This
means that software engineers are often involved in the entire software development life cycle,
including requirements analysis, design, coding, testing, deployment, and maintenance. On the other
hand, software developers typically focus more on the coding and implementation aspects of
software development.



Education: Software engineering is often considered a more formal discipline than software
development, and software engineers are usually expected to have a degree in computer science or a
related field. Software developers, on the other hand, may not necessarily have a formal education
in computer science, although many do.
Scope: Software engineering typically involves more complex projects and larger teams than software
development. Software engineers may work on projects that involve multiple software components or
systems, and they may need to coordinate the work of several developers. Software developers, on
the other hand, may work on smaller projects or individual components of larger projects.



In general, software engineering is a broader field that encompasses software development, and
software engineers may have a more holistic view of software development. Software developers, on the
other hand, may focus more on the coding and implementation aspects of software development.
